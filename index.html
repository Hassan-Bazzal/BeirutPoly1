About the Game

Beirut Poly is a city-themed board game where players buy, sell, and trade properties across Beirut’s iconic neighborhoods. From Hamra to Mar Mikhael, experience the chaos, charm, and culture of Lebanon’s capital through strategic gameplay and local satire.

Features

🏙️

Real Beirut Locations

Buy and trade neighborhoods like Gemmayzeh, Verdun, and Raouché.

💸

Dual Currency System

Play with LBP or USD, just like real life in Beirut.

🎲

Local Flavor

“Wasta” and “Mish Ma32oul” cards bring unexpected Lebanese twists.
